Extensions

Extensions are basically add-ons, but the extensions that we have on the Chromebooks are managed and monitored by HCPSS and teachers. This is easily exploitable using the 'managed extensions' extension against them. By scanning all extensions and seeing if they are managed, you can track down the extensions and toggle them off or on.

Be aware, these extensions automatically turn back on versions 128 and higher when you shut your computer, sign out, or when it sleeps (sleeping also counts as when you close your Chromebook).
