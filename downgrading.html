Downgrading

Downgrading is the concept of changing or returning to old versions. This can be useful because newer updates such as 128 or 129 have patched basically all exploits (not all, but most). Downgrading to versions such as 123, 124, or 125 can provide numerous exploits. In our opinion, the best version to downgrade to would be 125, attaining most exploits on that version.

Hi

In order to downgrade you need a specific type of USB. Even if you manage to downgrade if you do not inject certain keys quickly, an alert will sends to HCPSS that your version went down, not up.
